Feedback on PESE (Career Skills) Classes

Throughout the 7–8 PESE sessions, I had the opportunity to participate in a range of activities designed to enhance communication and professional skills. While the sessions were valuable, I believe there is potential to make them even more impactful and engaging for students like myself.

Session Structure and Participation

Group Discussions (GDs) proved to be highly effective in building confidence and communication skills. I recommend incorporating more interactive elements, such as regular GDs and collaborative activities, to encourage active participation from all students.

Suggested Improvements

Short, focused GDs and real-world scenarios can be included in every session, allowing everyone to practice and improve their speaking and critical thinking skills.

Activity Relevance

Replacing less relevant activities with skill-building exercises—such as mock interviews, presentations, and role-playing—would better prepare us for professional challenges.

Overall Assessment

The PESE classes have laid a strong foundation, and with a few adjustments, they can become even more beneficial for students preparing to enter the professional world. I appreciate the efforts of the instructors and look forward to more interactive and skill-oriented sessions in the future.

Thank you for considering this feedback. I am confident that these enhancements will make the PESE sessions more engaging and valuable for all students.
