Group Discussion Session

Topic: "Is Reservation in India Good or Bad?"

During the Career Skills (PESE) lab, we engaged in a group discussion on the topic "Is reservation in India good or bad?" The session encouraged diverse viewpoints and highlighted the importance of respectful debate and active listening.

My Contribution

I initiated the discussion by providing historical context and emphasized the need for reservation to address social and economic disparities. I also highlighted the various forms of reservation, including those for women and economically weaker sections.

Discussion Dynamics

While opinions varied, I aimed to maintain a balanced perspective and encouraged others to share their thoughts. This experience taught me the value of both assertiveness and inclusivity in group settings.

Learning Experience

The GD session was a valuable learning opportunity, helping me strengthen my argumentation skills and appreciate the importance of collaborative dialogue in professional environments.
